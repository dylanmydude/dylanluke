about

Dylan Luke

My name is Dylan. I'm based in the East Rand, East of Johannesburg, and I have been taking photos since 2012. My photographic fervour really began one year later when I found myself on a hiking trail in Crystal Springs during mushroom season. This profound experience enveloped me in the art of photography and I've been hooked ever since.

I have found that I enjoy photographing nature, people, and architecture the most, all oblivious to my being there, especially on misty foggy mornings. These are the moments I love to capture and share.

In addition to still images, I also create videos. They often have an obscure and slightly melancholic vibe that adds an interesting layer to my portfolio. This is my perspective, my journey in the world of visual storytelling.
