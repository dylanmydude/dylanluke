about

Dylan Luke

My name is Dylan. I'm based in the East of Johannesburg, I have been taking photos since 2012. My photographic fervour really began one year later when I found myself on a hiking trail in Crystal Springs during mushroom season.

I have found that I enjoy photographing nature, people, and architecture the most, all oblivious to my being there, especially on misty foggy mornings.
